Problem: Divisibility

Print all integers ai such that ai is divisible by x and not divisible by y, where 1 < ai < n < 100000. Input First, you are given t (t<100) - the number of test cases. In each of the following t lines, 3 integers: n x y. You might assume also that x < n and x is not divisible by y. Output In each of the following t lines, numbers requested in the problem description in the separated by a single space in ascending order. Example: Input: 2 7 2 4 35 5 12 Output: 2 6 5 10 15 20 25 30
